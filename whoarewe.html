Who Are We?

Get to know the team, based here in East London, UK. With a combined experience of over 50 years in vertical farming, our team of experts is dedicated to helping you succeed in the world of sustainable agriculture.

Glenda Smyth

Founder & Lead Consultant

Glenda Smyth, founder and owner of SUNFARM, has 25 years of agricultural experience setting up urban and vertical farms across London and the UK. With a PhD in Biology from the University of East London, Glenda spent the first decade of her career working for Dyson as part of their agricultural division. She could see the future in vertical farming and began consulting in 2006. Twenty years later, SUNFARM Consultancy stands as one of the UK's most respected vertical farm consultancies.

Naomi Aguirra

Lead Mechanical Design Engineer

Naomi Aguirra is our Lead Mechanical Design Engineer, with over a decade of experience in controlled environment engineering and sustainable infrastructure design. Naomi oversees the technical delivery of every farm we build — from system specification and climate control architecture through to installation and commissioning. Her expertise spans hydroponic mechanics, smart automation, and energy-efficient systems design, ensuring that every SUNFARM facility is engineered to perform from day one and improve over time.

Ines Correia

Operational Strategy Manager

Ines is our Operational Strategy Manager, specialising in helping clients build farming operations that are efficient, commercially resilient, and ready to scale. With a background in agricultural business management and supply chain optimisation, Ines works closely with clients to develop crop plans, labour structures, and performance frameworks tailored to their specific markets and facilities. Her focus is on ensuring that every farm SUNFARM delivers grows well, runs well, and is set up for long-term success.

Ruby Chen

Lead AI & Data Systems Analyst

Ruby is our Lead AI & Data Systems Analyst, responsible for the design, integration, and ongoing optimisation of the smart technology platforms that power every SUNFARM facility. With a background in data science and precision agriculture, Ruby translates raw sensor data into actionable insight, fine-tuning the AI models that manage climate control, irrigation, and yield forecasting across our client portfolio. Her work ensures that every SUNFARM system continues to learn, adapt, and improve with every growing cycle.
