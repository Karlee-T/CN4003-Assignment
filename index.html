SUNFARM provides the strategic, technical and operational know-how to get vertical farms of any scale a reality.
From development to optimisations in a controlled environment, we help growers, investors and companies build resilient and functional farming systems. With the clever use of AI to monitor and control growing conditions, optimal yields and quality year-round is ensured.

As global demand for locally grown, sustainable produce continues to rise, the farms of tomorrow are already being built today.
This emerging industry is transforming the way we grow food, and SUNFARM is at the forefront of this exciting revolution.
